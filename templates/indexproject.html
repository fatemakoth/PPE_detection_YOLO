About
PPE Safety Kit Object Detection

The PPE Guard detects Personal protective equipment safety kits. The model identifies 8 classes: Hardhat, NO-Hardhat, Mask, NO-Mask, Safety Vest, NO-Safety Vest and Person. By leveraging real-time detection capabilities, this system can be applied in industries such as construction, manufacturing, and warehousing to reduce safety risks, automate compliance monitoring, and support proactive decision-making.

Trained on 2,600+ annotated images from Roboflow. Validation: 114 images. Test: 82 images.
